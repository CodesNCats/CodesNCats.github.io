September 2011 - UNCF - The Art of Giving Back

  • The Art of Giving Back was developed from the vision of Philip Smallwood as an art exhibition to raise funds to benefit the UNCF (United Negro College Fund). The inaugural event was held in September of 2011 at the School of Visual Arts in NYC. The works of contemporary emerging, mid-career and established artists were featured for this inaugural event.
