Welcome to P Smallwood Prints                                              

P. Smallwood is an award-winning watercolor artist who has been painting and exhibiting his work for over 15 years. He refers to his portraiture as Lifescapes because of their visual storytelling abilities. Please enjoy Philip's original watercolors and limited edition Giclee prints.

Philip Smallwood can also create a special commissioned portrait for your family or business, please contact us if you are interested. Please join the P. Smallwood Art mailing list to be informed of new works, exhibitions and news.
