Directions to P. Smallwood's Gallery Showing

From the North off the Tapanzee Bridge and the Garden State Parkway heading south:

  • Over the Tapanzee Bridge, head east on I-287 E/I-87-S

  • Take Exit 14A for Garden State parkway toward NJ

  • Continue onto Garden State Parkway

  • Take Exit 165 toward Oradell

  • Merge onto Oradell Avenue

  • Turn right onto Grant Avenue, which turns into the Boulevard

  • Turn left onto Monmouth Avenue

  • Turn right onto Cypress Street

  • Turn left onto Cypress Court

  • Proceed to 435 Cypress Court

From the George Washington Bridge (GWB)

  • Head west on I-95 South

  • Take the NJ -4/I-95 South exit

  • Keep right to continue to Exit 72A and follow signs for NJ 4W/Paramus

  • Continue onto NJ 4W/Paramus

  • Take the River Road exit toward Teaneck/Bogota/New Milford

  • Turn right toward River Road

  • Turn left onto River Road

  • Turn right at major traffic light onto Roemer Avenue which turns into New Bridge Road

  • Turn left at the first light onto Boulevard

  • Turn right onto Henley Avenue

  • Turn left onto Cypress Street

  • Turn right onto Cypress Court

  • Turn left at the first light onto Boulevard
  • Turn right onto Henley Avenue
